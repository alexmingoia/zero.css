ZeroCSS

  • Simple, functional styling without classes.
  • Attributes for spacing and alignment.
  • Less than 10kb and 100% free.

Usage

Include links to the reset and zero.css:

<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/sindresorhus/modern-normalize@v1.1.0/modern-normalize.css" type="text/css" rel="stylesheet" />
<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/alexmingoia/zero.css@2023-07-09/zero.min.css" type="text/css" rel="stylesheet" />

Attributes

Layout

  • container sets max-width="sm" and margin-x="auto"

Alignment

  • distribute="start|center|across|end"
  • align="start|center|end"
  • direction="horizontal|vertical"
  • expand fills available space

Text

  • text-type="serif|sans-serif|monospace"
  • text-align="left|center|right"
  • text-size="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl|3xl|4xl"
  • text-color="muted|base|emphasis"
  • text-weight="100|200|300|400|500|600|700|800|900"

Spacing

  • max-width="none|2xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-horizontal="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-vertical="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-top="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-left="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-right="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • padding-bottom="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • margin="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • margin-horizontal="auto|xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
  • margin-vertical="xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl"
